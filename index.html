About Us

Oliyappurathu Stores proudly stands as a distinguished and reputable firm, setting the standard in the dynamic realm of construction material supply. Our unwavering commitment extends to meeting both wholesale and retail demands, providing an unparalleled selection of top-quality materials to builders, contractors, and individuals alike. With an expansive array of offerings, our inventory encompasses a comprehensive range of essential construction components. From the foundational strength of cement to the vibrant hues of paints, the durability of steels, and the versatility of aluminum sheets and rods – we curate a diverse catalog tailored to meet the unique needs of our valued customers. Our firm's dedication to excellence has solidified our position as a one-stop destination for those in pursuit of superior construction materials. Whether you're embarking on a grand-scale construction project or undertaking a smaller venture, Oliyappurathu Stores is committed to providing not just products but comprehensive solutions. Beyond the products themselves, we take pride in fostering a customer-centric approach. Our experienced team is ready to assist and guide you, ensuring that your specific requirements are not just met but exceeded. The legacy of Oliyappurathu Stores spans decades, and under the visionary leadership of O.M Jacob, we continue to uphold our reputation for reliability, trust, and quality in the market. Explore the wealth of possibilities at Oliyappurathu Stores – where excellence meets diversity, and your construction needs find a home.
